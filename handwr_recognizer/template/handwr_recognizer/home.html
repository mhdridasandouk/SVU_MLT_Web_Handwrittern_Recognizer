✍️ Handwritten Digit Recognizer

This project implements a classical, end-to-end handwritten digit recognition system. The pipeline takes an arbitrary input image — a single cropped digit, a photo of a form, or a scanned strip of digits — segments the individual digits, normalises each to the canonical MNIST format, and classifies them with a Support Vector Machine (SVM).

  • Upload a clear photo of handwritten digits.
  • Click Predict Numbers to see the digits found.
  • Click Predict & Show Image to also view the annotated result.
